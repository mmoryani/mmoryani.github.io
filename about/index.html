● The founder

Founder & den mother Boston, MA

She spent 8 years on the other side of the table.

Priyanki Amroliwala has spent eight years running doctor recruiting at one of New England's larger dental groups. Her job, every day: find new dentists, read their contracts, and get them hired. She has read thousands of first contracts — from the hiring side.

She chairs the ADSO HR Study Club. She sits on the Group Dentistry Now editorial board. Around 30,000 people follow her on LinkedIn, most of them in dental hiring. She knows exactly how the machine works, because she helped run it.

FirstFill is her switching sides. Same knowledge, same network, same relationships — now pointed at the people being recruited instead of the people recruiting. That's the whole origin story. No pivot deck, no rebrand. Just a recruiter who decided the students deserved the home-field advantage for once.

House rules — non-negotiable

The rules.

Pinned to the door
01

Verified members only.

School email or license. No anonymous accounts, no lurkers, no bots. Everyone in the room is who they say they are.

02

Free for members, forever.

Not free-for-now. Not free-until-funding. Free, forever. It is the founding promise of the club.

03

Employers can never DM you.

There is no toggle to flip, no setting to forget. The door does not exist. Your inbox belongs to members.

04

Employers pay for reach, never for your data.

They pay to post jobs and sponsor events. Your profile, your messages, your habits are not for sale. Ever.

05

What happens at events lives in the app.

Photo walls stay inside the club, not on public social. You can be a person here without performing for the internet.

That's it. Five rules.
Break one, we broke the club.
