§ 01 · About this session

What you're walking into.

Themed AMA. Bring a redacted offer letter; Priyanki reads it live with the group. For D4s and Y0 grads.

Format: 75 minutes, themed. Bring a redacted offer letter or post your question in advance — Priyanki triages and answers the highest-signal ones live. Camera-on for the first 50, watch-and-chat for everyone else. Dr. Anna Kim joins the second half to weigh in on ortho-specific contract questions.
