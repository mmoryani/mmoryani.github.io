§01 The terms

You're a guest in their house. Act like it.

Members built the trust. You get to borrow it — carefully.

You can
  • ＋
    Post roles.Full role cards on the jobs board, read by verified D1–D4s and new grads.
  • ＋
    Sponsor the good stuff.Events, cohorts, and The First Fill Show podcast. Your name where members already are.
  • ＋
    Get a brand page.A verified profile members can find when they're curious about you.
You can never
  • ✕
    DM members.Not once. Not "just to introduce yourself." Messaging is members-only.
  • ✕
    Buy their data.Not for sale. Not at any price. Don't ask twice.
  • ✕
    See the rooms.Messages, groups, the feed — what members say to each other stays with them.

These rules are why members trust the job board. And exactly why your post gets read.
