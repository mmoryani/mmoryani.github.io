FirstFill original NOV 2025 · Boston, MA

The founding dinner

A long table in the North End

Twelve seats: four D3s, three D4s, two year-one grads, a resident, a practice owner, and Priyanki. The rule was one phone at the table — the one taking the photos. Every product decision in the app traces back to an argument from this dinner, including the employers-can't-DM rule, which passed unanimously.
