Women in dentistry brunch

Private room · Harvard Square · Cambridge, MA

Fifty-six percent of dental grads are women. This brunch is where the maternity-language conversation started — the one that's now pinned in the app.

◆ HOW IT WENT

Three practice owners, two residents, and twenty students talked through the contract questions nobody teaches: maternity language, production-threshold resets, the difference between a practice that says "family friendly" and one that writes it into the contract. Priyanki's pinned post in #female-grads is the distilled version.
