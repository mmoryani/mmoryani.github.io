Before you ask

Fine print, minus the fine.

Is it really free? +

Yes. Employers pay for job posts and sponsorships — that’s the business. Members never pay a cent, and there is no premium tier waiting to ambush you later.

Who can join? +

US dental students (D1 through D4), dentists in their first 5 years of practice, and verified mentors. That’s the whole guest list.

Can my program director or a recruiter see my messages? +

No. DMs are between members, full stop. Employers can’t DM anyone — the feature doesn’t exist for them — and nobody outside a conversation can read it.

What school emails work? +

Any ADA-accredited US dental school .edu address. Already graduated? You verify with your NPI number instead — takes the same 90 seconds.
